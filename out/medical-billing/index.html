Full Revenue Cycle Management Services

Every step of the billing cycle handled end-to-end — from the moment a patient is seen to the final dollar collected.

01

Verification of Benefits

Verify insurance eligibility and coverage before every claim. Confirm prior authorizations or referrals required by payer.

02

Medical Coding (ICD-10, CPT, HCPCS)

Accurate code assignment based on clinical documentation. Full compliance with payer guidelines and CMS regulations.

03

Charge Entry & Clean Claim Submission

Accurate charge entry with correct codes, modifiers, provider and facility data. Electronic and paper claim submission with rejection monitoring.

04

Payment Posting & Reconciliation

Post payments from EOBs/ERAs. Apply contractual adjustments, co-pays, deductibles, and write-offs. Reconcile with bank deposits.

05

A/R Follow-Up & Denial Management

Track unpaid and denied claims. Timely follow-up with payers. Root cause denial analysis, appeals preparation, and resubmission.

06

Patient Billing & Support

Accurate patient statements, billing inquiry handling, payment plan management, and friendly balance clarification.

07

Reporting & KPI Analytics

Weekly/monthly performance reports tracking collection rate, aging A/R, denial trends, and revenue optimisation insights.

08

HIPAA Compliance & Data Security

Strict HIPAA compliance, regular billing audits, clean coding practices, and secure systems protecting patient data at all times.
